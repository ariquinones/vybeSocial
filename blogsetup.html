Blog Content Creation Services

Blog setup

A great blog for any business attracts traffic to your site and converts that traffic into customers. A blog also empowers your fans and customers with the knowledge they need to get the most out of your product or service.

Blogging gives your business a voice that your audience can rely on. It is the best SEO strategy for every online business in today's digital market.


So where do you start?


There are two main bodies of work that go into creating a successful blog campaign. The first of which is creating and setting up the blog pages on your existing site.

  1. Have a content management system in place
  2. Template your blog post graphics
  3. Template your blog content so that it's easy to add to your ongoing campaign

The other half is far less technical, but ongoing; which is creating a streamlined process to repeat each time you post a new blog.

  1. Defining your audience
  2. Determining your keywords
  3. Compiling a list of topics worthy of blogging about
  4. Creating a post schedule
  5. Building the right team of writers and editors who post and shares your content online

Publishing relevant content on a consistent basis for your audience will position your company as an industry leader and will help you showcase your expertise.
